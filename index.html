About this website


Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, Kottayam is a government engineering college owned and operated by the Government of Kerala. Located 14 km from Kottayam near Pampady, the institute was founded in 1991 with the view of making it a centre of post-graduate and research studies. Spanning over more than 90 acres, the lush green campus consists of departments like Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering and Architecture.

ritmaintenance.com provides an interactive platform to students and faculty of RIT alike, to report and describe problems associated with amenities like electricity, water(drinking and general use), toilet, general cleanliness, desks and benches, class supplies, projectors etc. the reports are sent to the concerned authorities who will take action regarding each issue as fast as possible. ritmaintenance.com expands it services to all the departments of RIT. In addition to this, it also provides services to college hostels LH and MH; and also ground and canteen
